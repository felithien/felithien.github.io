SATE

(ID) SATE
(EN) SATAY
(CH) 沙爹
Satay may consist of diced or sliced chicken, goat, mutton, beef, pork, fish, other meats, or tofu. Satay can be served in various sauces, however most often they are served in a combination of soy and peanut sauce.
RAWON

(ID) RAWON
(EN) RAWON
(CH) 拉甕
Rawon is a strong rich tasting traditional Indonesian beef black soup. It uses black nuts/keluak (Pangeum edule, fruits of kepayang tree) as the main seasoning which gives the strong nutty flavor and dark color to the soup.
PEMPEK

(ID) PEMPEK
(EN) PEMPEK
(CH) 「伯伯」魚餅
Pempek, mpek-mpek or empek-empek is a savoury Indonesian fishcake delicacy, made of fish and tapioca, from Palembang, South Sumatera, Indonesia. Pempek is served with rich sweet and sour sauce called kuah cuka or kuah cuko (lit. vinegar sauce)

GADO-GADO

(ID) GADO-GADO
(EN) GADO-GADO
(CH) 加多加多
Gado-gado is an Indonesian salad of slightly boiled, blanched or steamed vegetables and hard-boiled eggs, boiled potato, fried tofu and tempeh, and lontong (rice wrapped in a banana leaf), served with a peanut sauce dressing.
KOLAK

(ID) KOLAK
(EN) KOLAK
(CH) 科拉克
Kolak is Indonesian dessert made with palm sugar and coconut milk, with pandanus leaf for flavour. Usually, banana and sweet potatoes are added in it.
NASI GORENG

(ID) NASI GORENG
(EN) FRIED RICE
(CH) 炒飯
RENDANG

(ID) RENDANG
(EN) RENDANG
(CH) 仁當
Rendang is an Indonesian spicy meat dish originating from the Minangkabau people of Indonesia. Along with the main meat ingredient, rendang uses coconut milk and a paste of mixed ground spices, including ginger, galangal, turmeric leaves, lemongrass, garlic, shallots, chillis and other spices.
SOTO

(ID) SOTO
(EN) SOTO
(CH) 素都
Soto is a traditional Indonesian soup mainly composed of broth, meat, and vegetables. It is usually accompanied by rice or compressed rice cakes (lontong, ketupat or burasa).
TEMPE

(ID) TEMPE
(EN) TEMPE (FERMENTED SOYA BEAN)
(CH) 天貝
Tempe (/ˈtɛmpeɪ/) is a traditional Indonesian soy product, originating from Indonesia.It is made by a natural culturing and controlled fermentation process that binds soybeans into a cake form.Here a special fungus is used, which has the Latin name Rhizopus oligosporus.
